YSE130 Dual Motor Electric Wheelchair | Freedom, Comfort & Portability

Published on: 2026-2-06 | Author: Iyasocare

For many people with mobility challenges, the right wheelchair isn’t just a tool—it’s a gateway to independence. The YSE130 Dual Motor Electric Wheelchair is designed to give users freedom, comfort, and confidence wherever life takes them. Lightweight, foldable, and airline-approved, it’s perfect for home, city streets, and even travel adventures.

Comfort That Keeps You Moving

The YSE130 isn’t just functional—it’s built for comfort. Its 44cm wide, 45cm deep seat and 8cm thick cushion provide plush support, while the 43cm high backrest ensures proper posture throughout the day. The flip-up armrests make getting in and out effortless, offering practical convenience for daily life and outings alike.

With thoughtful design like this, every ride feels smoother and more relaxed, whether you’re navigating indoors or exploring new destinations.

Power and Performance You Can Trust

Equipped with dual 250W motors and a 24V 12Ah battery, the YSE130 can travel up to 15 kilometers on a single charge at a top speed of 6 km/h. Its climbing ability of 15° makes hills and ramps manageable, giving users confidence in varied environments.

The wheelchair also features an electric parking brake and a smart controller for safe, precise handling. When needed, switching to manual mode is quick and easy—perfect for tight spaces or emergency situations.

Travel-Ready Design

One of the biggest advantages of the YSE130 is its foldable, lightweight frame. Weighing just 32 kg, it folds down to 756234 cm, making it easy to store in a car trunk, under an airplane seat, or even in compact home spaces.

This makes the YSE130 ideal for frequent travelers who refuse to let mobility limitations get in the way. From weekend trips to international flights, it’s a dependable companion for life on the move.

Airline Approved for Stress-Free Travel

The YSE130 is airline-approved, meaning you can bring it on board without hassle. This small but important detail allows users to travel confidently, knowing their mobility device meets airline requirements. It’s freedom you can take anywhere.

Specifications at a Glance

  • Seat Dimensions: 44cm W × 45cm D × 50cm H
  • Backrest Height: 43cm
  • Wheelchair Dimensions: 946289cm | Folded: 756234cm
  • Wheels: Rear 12” | Front 8”
  • Maximum Load: 100 kg
  • Charging Time: 4–6 hours
  • Driving Range: ~15 km
  • Top Speed: 6 km/h
  • Climbing Ability: 15°

Why Choose the YSE130

The YSE130 isn’t just a wheelchair—it’s a lifestyle upgrade. By combining comfort, power, portability, and travel readiness, it allows users to live without limits, whether at home, in the city, or abroad.

If you’re looking for a lightweight, foldable, dual-motor wheelchair that can go anywhere, the YSE130 is your perfect solution. Take control of your mobility and rediscover the joy of going wherever you want—without compromise.
