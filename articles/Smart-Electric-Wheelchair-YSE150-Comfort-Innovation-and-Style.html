Smart Electric Wheelchair YSE150 Comfort Innovation and Style

Published on: 2026-01-28 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility meets innovation with the Smart Electric Wheelchair YSE150, designed to bring freedom, comfort, and elegance to daily life. Whether you’re navigating city streets, home corridors, or outdoor pathways, the YSE150 combines cutting-edge technology with thoughtful design.

Sleek Design and Premium Build

The YSE150 stands out with its aluminum alloy frame, providing both durability and lightweight maneuverability. Weighing only 30 kg, it’s easy to transport while capable of supporting up to 130 kg. The wheelchair’s compact dimensions (1025599 cm) ensure effortless navigation even in tight spaces.

The seat is 40 cm wide and 44 cm deep, with a height of 55 cm, offering comfortable support for long-term use. Complementing the ergonomic seat, the 3D butterfly wing cushion backrest brings superior lumbar support and a unique aesthetic touch, making comfort a priority.

Smooth Mobility and Powerful Performance

Equipped with a 250W dual motor system, the YSE150 can reach a maximum speed of 6 km/h and conquer inclines up to 10°, giving you the confidence to move freely both indoors and outdoors. The 8-inch McNamee front wheels and 9-inch rear wheels provide stability and a smooth ride over various surfaces.

The wheelchair’s 24V lithium battery comes in 8/12/20A options, offering a driving range of 15-25 km on a full charge. With a charging time of 4-6 hours, the YSE150 ensures you’re ready to go when you need it.

Advanced Features for Safety and Convenience

Safety and convenience are at the heart of the YSE150’s design. Key features include:

  • Fully automatic electromagnetic parking brake for secure stops every time.
  • Intelligent controller with built-in lighting function for visibility and ease of use.
  • Adjustable seat height, telescopic footrest, and foldable armrests for customized comfort.
  • Red or white cushion options to match your personal style.

Portability and Ease of Use

When it comes to transport, the YSE150 doesn’t disappoint. With a net/gross weight of 30/35 kg and a packing size of 1055850 cm, it’s compact enough to fit in most vehicles. The foldable design and lightweight frame make it easy to carry, store, and travel with.

Why Choose the YSE150

The Smart Electric Wheelchair YSE150 is more than just a mobility device—it’s a lifestyle upgrade. From advanced comfort features like the 3D butterfly wing cushion to powerful motor performance and safety innovations, it’s designed for users who refuse to compromise on style, mobility, or independence.

Experience the perfect balance of comfort, technology, and design with the YSE150 and rediscover the joy of moving freely.
