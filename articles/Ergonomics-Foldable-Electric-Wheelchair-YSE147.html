Ergonomic Foldable Electric Wheelchair YSE147

Published on: 2026-03-12 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility is more than movement—it’s independence, confidence, and the ability to live life on your terms. For individuals who face daily mobility challenges, the right wheelchair can transform experiences, restore freedom, and improve quality of life. The Ergonomic Foldable Electric Wheelchair YSE147 is engineered to do exactly that. Combining modern technology, ergonomic design, and travel-friendly convenience, it’s a game-changer in mobility solutions.

Whether navigating city streets, exploring parks, or moving comfortably around the home, the YSE147 offers a blend of power, comfort, and style that empowers every user. Below is a detailed look at the features and advantages that make this electric wheelchair a reliable choice for modern mobility needs.

Ergonomic Design: The Ultimate in Comfort

Comfort is one of the most important aspects of wheelchair design. The YSE147 prioritizes ergonomic support to ensure a comfortable riding experience even during extended use.

  • Seat Dimensions: 40 cm width, 40 cm depth, and 48 cm height for balanced seating support.
  • Backrest & Headrest: A 50 cm backrest with a 36 cm adjustable headrest supports spinal alignment and neck comfort.
  • Advanced Cushioning: Second-generation ergonomic backrest combined with removable leather cushions for enhanced comfort.

This thoughtful ergonomic design minimizes pressure points and helps users remain comfortable throughout the day.

Lightweight Yet Durable: Aluminum Frame Construction

The YSE147 balances durability with portability through its lightweight aluminum frame.

  • Aluminum Frame: Strong yet lightweight for daily reliability.
  • Net & Gross Weight: 23 kg net weight and 25 kg gross weight.
  • Load Capacity: Supports users up to 100 kg.

This construction ensures durability while keeping the wheelchair easy to handle and transport.

Foldable Design: Travel-Friendly Mobility

The YSE147 folds into a compact size of 97 × 59 × 60 cm, making storage and transport extremely convenient. Whether placed in a car trunk, airplane cargo compartment, or small home storage space, the foldable design makes mobility more practical for modern lifestyles.

Dual Motor System: Power Meets Efficiency

The wheelchair is powered by dual 180W motors, delivering consistent performance across different environments.

  • Maximum Speed: 6 km/h
  • Climbing Ability: Up to 9° slopes
  • Driving Range: 5–20 km depending on battery capacity

This motor system ensures smooth acceleration, reliable performance, and stable navigation both indoors and outdoors.

Innovative Wheel Technology for Maximum Stability

  • Front Wheels: 8-inch PU wheels with built-in shock absorption.
  • Rear Wheels: 12-inch honeycomb run-flat tires that are explosion-proof and puncture-resistant.

The wheel system enhances stability and reduces vibration, allowing users to travel comfortably across various surfaces.

Customizable Comfort for Every User

The YSE147 includes adjustable features that accommodate individual preferences.

  • Rear Flip Armrests: Padded PU armrests lift for easy transfers.
  • Adjustable Legrests: Provide natural leg positioning.
  • Configurable Headrest: Adjustable and padded for long rides.

These adjustable components ensure each user can personalize the wheelchair to suit their comfort needs.

Battery and Safety Features

  • Battery Options: 24V 6Ah, 10Ah, or 20Ah lithium batteries.
  • Charging Time: Approximately 4–6 hours.
  • Electromagnetic Parking Brake: Prevents unintended movement.

These safety features enhance confidence and reliability during daily use.

Stylish and Functional Design

The YSE147 combines performance with modern aesthetics.

  • Spraying sand black aluminum frame for a sleek appearance.
  • Removable leather cushions for comfort and easy maintenance.

The design ensures the wheelchair looks as good as it performs.

Ideal for Multiple Lifestyles

The versatility of the YSE147 makes it suitable for a wide range of situations:

  • Indoor use at home or in offices
  • Outdoor mobility in parks and urban environments
  • Travel and transportation thanks to the foldable frame

Maintenance Tips

  • Charge the battery regularly and avoid deep discharge.
  • Keep wheels clean to maintain smooth operation.
  • Inspect motors, joystick, and braking system periodically.
  • Clean leather cushions with a soft damp cloth.

Proper maintenance ensures the wheelchair performs reliably for years.

Conclusion: Empower Your Independence

The Ergonomic Foldable Electric Wheelchair YSE147 is more than a mobility device—it’s a gateway to independence and confidence. Its ergonomic design, dual-motor power, lightweight construction, and travel-friendly foldability create a perfect balance of comfort and functionality.

For users seeking reliable mobility solutions that combine technology, style, and convenience, the YSE147 represents a powerful step toward a more independent and active lifestyle.
