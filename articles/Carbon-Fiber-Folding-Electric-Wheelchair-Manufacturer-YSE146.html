Why Carbon Fiber Is Revolutionizing Electric Wheelchairs

Traditional electric wheelchairs are often built from steel or aluminum alloys. While these materials provide durability, they can also add significant weight, making transportation and storage challenging.

Carbon fiber has become increasingly popular because it offers several important advantages:

  • Exceptional strength-to-weight ratio
  • Corrosion resistance
  • Long-term durability
  • Reduced overall wheelchair weight
  • Easier handling for caregivers
  • Improved portability for travel

The YSE146 utilizes a high-strength carbon fiber frame that significantly reduces weight while maintaining structural integrity and user safety. This allows users to enjoy greater independence without sacrificing performance.

Ultra-Lightweight Design for Everyday Convenience

One of the biggest challenges facing wheelchair users is transportation.

Whether loading the wheelchair into a vehicle, navigating airports, or storing it at home, weight matters. The YSE146 addresses this challenge with a remarkably lightweight design.

The wheelchair weighs approximately:

  • 15 kg without battery
  • 23 kg with battery installed

Compared with many traditional power wheelchairs, this reduction in weight makes lifting and handling significantly easier for users and caregivers alike.

For active individuals who frequently travel or attend social activities, a lightweight folding electric wheelchair can dramatically improve daily mobility and convenience.

Powerful Dual Brushless Motor System

A lightweight wheelchair is only effective if it delivers reliable performance.

The YSE146 is equipped with dual 24V 200W brushless motors that provide smooth and efficient power output. Brushless motors offer several advantages over traditional brushed motors:

Longer Service Life

Brushless motors experience less wear because they contain fewer friction-based components.

Lower Maintenance Requirements

Reduced mechanical wear means fewer maintenance issues over time.

Quieter Operation

Users benefit from a smoother and quieter driving experience in both indoor and outdoor environments.

Improved Energy Efficiency

Brushless technology helps maximize battery performance and driving range.

Together, the dual motors provide stable propulsion for everyday mobility tasks, shopping trips, community outings, and recreational travel.

Long Battery Range for Greater Independence

Range anxiety is a common concern among electric wheelchair users.

The YSE146 addresses this issue with a 24V 20Ah lithium battery system capable of delivering an estimated driving range of 20–30 kilometers on a single charge.

This extended range allows users to:

  • Enjoy outdoor activities
  • Travel throughout the day without frequent charging
