YSE111 Portable Lightweight Electric Wheelchair

Published on: 2026-01-22 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility is more than just moving from one place to another—it’s about independence, comfort, and confidence. The YSE111 Portable Lightweight Electric Wheelchair is designed to give you all that and more, combining innovative design with practical functionality.

Travel Light, Move Freely

With its lightweight folding design, the YSE111 makes travel effortless. Whether you’re hopping in a car, taking public transport, or exploring new places, this wheelchair folds compactly to 1184677 cm, saving space without compromising comfort.

Strong, Stylish, and Built to Last

Crafted with a magnesium alloy frame and finished in sleek black matte, the YSE111 is both durable and stylish. It’s engineered to support daily use while maintaining a modern, elegant look.

Power Meets Performance

Equipped with dual 250W motors, the YSE111 reaches a top speed of 6 km/h and a range of 15 km on a single charge. Its climbing ability of up to 15° makes it perfect for handling everyday obstacles. Switching to manual mode is easy—just 3 seconds and you’re in full control.

Comfort Designed for You

Your comfort matters. The YSE111 features a 43 cm wide seat, 48 cm seat depth, 48 cm seat height, and a 41 cm backrest, along with flip-up armrests and detachable footrests for maximum flexibility. Choose from Black, Red, or Blue cushions to match your style.

Safe and User-Friendly

Safety is a top priority. The YSE111 comes with manual and electronic parking brakes, ensuring stability wherever you stop. Its foldable handles and lightweight design make everyday use simple and convenient.

Why Choose YSE111?

The YSE111 Portable Lightweight Electric Wheelchair delivers mobility, style, and independence all in one package. Whether it’s for daily life, travel, or outdoor adventures, it empowers users to move confidently and comfortably.

Take charge of your mobility and enjoy life on your terms with the YSE111—freedom has never been this easy.
