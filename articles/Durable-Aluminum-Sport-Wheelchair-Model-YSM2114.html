Durable Aluminum Sport Wheelchair (Model YSM2114)

Published on: 2026-05-19 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility solutions today are no longer limited to basic functionality. Modern wheelchairs are designed with ergonomics, lightweight materials, and sport-ready performance in mind. The Durable Aluminum Sport Wheelchair Model YSM2114 is a strong example of this evolution—combining portability, durability, and comfort in a well-balanced design suitable for everyday use and active lifestyles.

This overview takes a deep dive into its structure, features, and real-world usability so you can understand what makes this model stand out.

1. Lightweight Aluminum Frame for Easy Mobility

One of the defining features of this model is its aluminum alloy frame. Aluminum is widely used in mobility equipment because it offers an excellent balance between strength and weight.

At a net weight of approximately 12.9 kg, this wheelchair is considered lightweight for its category. The reduced weight provides several practical advantages:

  • Easier self-propulsion for users
  • Less strain for caregivers when pushing or transporting
  • More convenient lifting into vehicles
  • Improved portability during travel

The folding design further enhances convenience, making it suitable for users who need a wheelchair that can be stored or transported frequently.

2. Compact Folding Design for Travel and Storage

This model features a folding frame mechanism, allowing it to collapse into a compact form:

Folding size: 85 × 28 × 88 cm

This compact structure makes it particularly useful in scenarios such as:

  • Car travel and road trips
  • Medical visits and hospital transport
  • Storage in small apartments or tight spaces
  • Airline or long-distance travel preparation

The folding process is designed to be simple and quick, ensuring users can transition between active use and storage without difficulty.

3. Ergonomic Seating for Long-Term Comfort

Comfort is a key factor in wheelchair design, and this model integrates thoughtful features to support long-term sitting.

Seat Configuration:

  • Seat width options: 36 / 38 / 40 cm
  • Seat depth: 40 cm
  • Seat height: 50 cm

This range of sizing options helps accommodate different body types, improving posture alignment and stability.

Cushioning System:

  • Oxford canvas upholstery
  • Sponge cushioning layer

This combination provides breathability, soft support for extended sitting periods, and reduced pressure points for improved comfort. The result is a seating experience that balances firmness and softness for both short trips and long-term use.

4. Butterfly-Shaped Backrest: Enhanced Support Design

A standout ergonomic feature is the butterfly-shaped backrest. Unlike flat or rigid backrests, this design:

  • Follows the natural curve of the back
  • Improves lumbar support
  • Reduces fatigue during long-term sitting
  • Provides a more stable leaning surface

This design upgrade significantly improves comfort for users who rely on the wheelchair daily.

5. Sport-Style Wheel System for Smooth Movement

The wheelchair is equipped with a dual-wheel system designed for both indoor and outdoor performance.

Rear Wheels: 24-inch inflatable sports wheels

  • Better shock absorption on uneven surfaces
  • Smoother movement outdoors
  • Improved self-propulsion efficiency

Front Wheels: 4-inch PU wheels

  • High durability
  • Low maintenance
  • Stable indoor maneuverability
  • Resistance to wear and punctures

Together, this configuration creates a balanced ride suitable for urban environments, parks, sidewalks, and indoor spaces.

6. Strong Load Capacity and Structural Stability

This model supports a maximum load of 100 kg, making it suitable for a wide range of users.

The aluminum frame contributes to structural rigidity under pressure, resistance to bending or deformation, and long-term durability with regular use. This ensures safety and reliability during daily activities such as commuting, shopping, or rehabilitation use.

7. Dimensions and Practical Usability

Overall product size: 85 × 55 × 89 cm

Rear wheel diameter: 24 inches

Front wheel diameter: 4 inches

These dimensions indicate a compact yet stable design, suitable for both tight indoor spaces and open outdoor environments.

8. Who Is This Wheelchair Best For?

This model is designed for a variety of users, including:

  • Individuals needing daily mobility support
  • Elderly users requiring stable and comfortable seating
  • Patients in rehabilitation or recovery phases
  • Active users seeking a sport-style wheelchair
  • Caregivers needing a lightweight and portable solution

Its combination of comfort, portability, and durability makes it a versatile choice across different lifestyles.

9. Final Thoughts

The Durable Aluminum Sport Wheelchair Model YSM2114 is more than just a mobility aid—it is a thoughtfully engineered solution designed to improve independence, comfort, and movement efficiency.

With its lightweight aluminum frame, foldable compact structure, sport-style wheel system, ergonomic seating, butterfly backrest, and durable materials, it stands out as a practical and user-friendly option for everyday mobility needs.

Whether used at home, outdoors, or during travel, this wheelchair delivers a balanced experience of comfort and performance.
