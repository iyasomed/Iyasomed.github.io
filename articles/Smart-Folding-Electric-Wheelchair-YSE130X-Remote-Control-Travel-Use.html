Smart Folding Electric Wheelchair YSE130X Remote Control Travel Use

Published on: 2026-04-16 | Author: Iyasocare

In today’s world of smart mobility solutions, electric wheelchairs are no longer just assistive devices—they are evolving into compact, intelligent, and travel-friendly personal transport systems. The YSE130X Smart Folding Electric Wheelchair (Remote Control Version) is designed with exactly this idea in mind: combining portability, convenience, and practical performance for both daily use and travel scenarios.

1. Product Positioning: Built for Travel and Portability

The YSE130X is a lightweight, foldable, travel-oriented electric wheelchair designed for users who need easy transportation, compact storage, airline-friendly mobility support, and remote control assistance. It focuses on mobility freedom and travel convenience.

2. Structure and Dimensions

Seating Comfort
Seat width: 48 cm
Seat depth: 45 cm
Seat height: 50 cm
Cushion thickness: 8 cm
Backrest height: 43 cm

The seating system provides balanced comfort and posture support. The 8 cm cushion improves pressure distribution for long-term sitting.

Overall Size and Folding Design
Unfolded size: 98 × 63 × 110 cm
Folded size: 78 × 63 × 48 cm

The folding design makes it easy to store in car trunks, carry during travel, and place in tight spaces.

Wheel Configuration
Rear wheels: 12 inches
Front wheels: 8 inches

This configuration improves maneuverability, stability, and obstacle handling.

3. Motor System and Performance

Dual Motor Power
250W × 2 dual motors provide smoother turning, stronger traction, and stable climbing performance.

Speed and Range
Maximum speed: 6 km/h
Driving range: 15–25 km

This is suitable for daily errands, short trips, and travel assistance.

Climbing Ability
Maximum incline: 15°

Supports ramps, parking slopes, and urban inclines.

4. Battery and Charging System

Battery: 24V 12Ah lithium battery
Charging time: 4–6 hours

Lithium battery advantages include lighter weight, longer lifespan, and faster charging. Charging via controller adds convenience.

5. Safety and Control System

Electromagnetic Braking System
Automatically locks when joystick is released and prevents rolling on slopes.

Remote Control Operation
Allows caregiver remote control, positioning assistance, and easy indoor handling.

Manual Mode Switching
Supports manual pushing during emergencies or low battery situations.

6. Comfort and User-Friendly Design

Flip-Up Armrests
Improve accessibility and side transfers.

Cushion and Back Support
8 cm cushion ensures comfort and ergonomic posture support.

Folding Mechanism
Quick folding for easy storage and transport.

7. Weight and Load Capacity

Net weight: 31 kg
Gross weight: 35 kg
Maximum load: 120 kg

Lightweight yet strong enough for most adult users.

8. Airline and Travel Compatibility

The YSE130X is designed for air travel compatibility (subject to airline rules), making it ideal for frequent travelers.

9. Ideal Use Cases

Daily Life: shopping, neighborhood mobility, indoor/outdoor short trips

Elderly Assistance: caregiver-supported movement

Medical Use: hospital transport and rehabilitation

Travel: airports, hotels, sightseeing

10. Advantages and Limitations

Advantages:
Dual motor stability
Compact folding design
Remote control operation
Lightweight frame
Electromagnetic braking
Travel-friendly structure

Limitations:
6 km/h speed is safety-limited
15–25 km range suits short trips
Seat width may not suit larger users

Conclusion

The YSE130X Smart Folding Electric Wheelchair focuses on portability, safety, and intelligent control. It is designed for users who value independence, travel convenience, and caregiver-friendly operation rather than heavy-duty performance.
