Electric Wheelchair YSE142 Compact, Lightweight, and Travel-Friendly Mobility Solution

Published on: 2026-03-06 | Author: Iyasocare

In today’s fast-paced world, mobility and independence are more important than ever. The YSE142 electric wheelchair is designed for individuals seeking convenience, comfort, and freedom of movement. Combining cutting-edge technology with a lightweight and foldable design, this wheelchair offers unmatched versatility both indoors and outdoors, making it an ideal companion for travel, daily use, and adventurous exploration.

Compact and Portable Design

One of the standout features of the YSE142 is its compact size. Measuring 92 cm in length, 60 cm in width, and 93 cm in height, this wheelchair is designed to navigate through tight spaces such as narrow doorways, crowded streets, and busy airports. When folded, it shrinks to 60 cm x 28 cm x 70 cm, making it easy to store in a car trunk, under a desk, or even in overhead compartments on planes. Its lightweight frame, constructed from magnalium, ensures that the wheelchair remains sturdy yet easy to carry, with a net weight of just 18.5 kg for the standard model and 22.5 kg for the upgraded version.

Comfortable Seating

The YSE142 doesn’t compromise on comfort. It features a seat width of 43 cm, a back height of 48 cm, and a seat depth of 44 cm, offering ample space for users of different body types. The black cushion provides a soft and supportive seating experience, reducing pressure and ensuring comfort during extended use. For added convenience, the wheelchair is equipped with flip-up armrests, allowing users to transfer in and out of the chair effortlessly.

Powerful Performance

Despite its compact size, the YSE142 is powered by a dual 150W brushless motor, delivering smooth and reliable propulsion. With a maximum speed of 6 km/h and a driving range of up to 15 km on a single charge, it is ideal for both short trips and longer excursions. The wheelchair can climb slopes of up to 9°, ensuring it can handle moderate inclines without difficulty.

Advanced Battery and Charging

The YSE142 features a 24V lithium battery with options for 6Ah or 12Ah, offering flexibility depending on the user’s range requirements. Charging is convenient and straightforward with a controller charging system, taking approximately 4–6 hours to reach full capacity. The efficient battery system ensures that users can rely on the wheelchair for daily use and travel without frequent interruptions for charging.

Travel-Ready Features

Designed with travelers in mind, the YSE142 can be carried on airplanes, thanks to its lightweight frame and compact folding design. Its manual mode allows users to switch seamlessly between electric and manual operation, providing additional flexibility when navigating areas where motorized operation isn’t allowed or practical. The wheelchair’s front 7-inch wheels and rear 10-inch wheels offer stability and smooth handling on various surfaces, from paved streets to slightly uneven terrain.

Easy Operation and Safety

The YSE142 is not only functional but also user-friendly. The intuitive control system allows even first-time users to operate it with ease. The lightweight foldable design means that users or caregivers can quickly fold and unfold the wheelchair, making transportation and storage effortless. Safety is prioritized with stable wheel construction, strong frame materials, and reliable braking mechanisms.

Conclusion

The YSE142 electric wheelchair is a versatile mobility solution for individuals who value independence, comfort, and portability. Its combination of lightweight design, foldable functionality, powerful motors, and long-range battery makes it perfect for daily use, travel, and exploring new places. Whether navigating city streets, going to work, or traveling by plane, the YSE142 provides a reliable, stylish, and convenient solution for modern mobility needs. With the YSE142, freedom of movement is no longer a compromise—it’s a lifestyle.
