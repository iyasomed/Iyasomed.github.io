Ergonomic Manual Wheelchair YSM2057 with Folding Frame and Commode

Published on: 2026-04-02 | Author: Iyasocare

Finding the perfect wheelchair means balancing comfort, durability, and convenience. The YSM2057 Manual Wheelchair is designed to meet all these needs, providing users with the freedom to move safely and confidently while offering caregivers ease of use. With its thoughtful design and practical features, this wheelchair is ideal for home, travel, and care settings alike.

Lightweight, Foldable, and Travel-Friendly

One of the most practical aspects of the YSM2057 is its lightweight aluminum alloy frame. Weighing only 13kg, this wheelchair is easy to lift, transport, and maneuver. Its foldable design reduces the size to 91×25×70cm, making it convenient to store in a car, closet, or small space.

The folding handle adds extra convenience, allowing caregivers to push or adjust the wheelchair effortlessly. This combination of portability and functionality makes the YSM2057 perfect for people on the go.

Designed for Comfort

Comfort is essential for anyone who spends time in a wheelchair. The YSM2057 features a luxurious sofa-style cushion and a backrest height of 40cm, offering excellent support for proper posture and reducing fatigue. The waterproof fabric makes cleaning simple and keeps the wheelchair hygienic, so users can enjoy comfort without hassle.

Whether it’s a short trip or a full day in the chair, the YSM2057 ensures a pleasant and supportive experience for users.

Smooth Ride and Stability

The YSM2057 delivers a smooth, stable ride thanks to its shock-absorbing PU wheels. The 6-inch front wheels allow easy navigation through tight spaces, while the 20-inch rear wheels provide stability and control. This setup ensures a smooth ride on different surfaces, whether indoors or outdoors.

The wheelchair safely supports up to 100kg, giving users and caregivers confidence in its durability and stability.

Practical, Everyday Features

Beyond mobility, the YSM2057 is designed to make daily life easier. It includes a bedpan (26×15×37cm) and a toilet hole (26×22cm), allowing users to manage personal care with minimal effort. These features make the wheelchair especially convenient for home care or healthcare environments.

  • Stable and smooth ride: Shock-absorbing PU wheels for indoor and outdoor use.

  • Reliable support: Supports up to 100kg safely.

Conclusion

The YSM2057 Manual Wheelchair is a reliable, comfortable, and versatile mobility solution. Its lightweight, foldable frame, luxurious cushioning, smooth ride, and practical features make it a perfect choice for users seeking independence and ease.

Whether for home use, travel, or healthcare settings, the YSM2057 provides comfort, safety, and confidence. Choose the YSM2057 and experience mobility without compromise.
