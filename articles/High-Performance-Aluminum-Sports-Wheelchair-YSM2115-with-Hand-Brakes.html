High-Performance Aluminum Sports Wheelchair YSM2115 with Hand Brakes

Published on: 2026-05-26 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility should never feel like limitation—it should feel like freedom engineered with precision. The YSM2115 High-Performance Aluminum Sports Wheelchair is designed around that idea, blending lightweight construction, ergonomic comfort, and practical control features into a single modern mobility solution.

Whether used for daily mobility support, rehabilitation, or active lifestyles, this model focuses on stability, comfort, and efficiency without unnecessary bulk.

A Lightweight Aluminum Frame Built for Real-World Use

One of the standout features of the YSM2115 is its aluminum lightweight frame, which balances durability with portability. Aluminum is widely valued in mobility equipment because it offers strong structural integrity, corrosion resistance, and significantly reduced weight compared to steel.

With a net weight of approximately 11 kg (15.5 kg gross), this wheelchair is easy to transport, store, and maneuver. For users and caregivers alike, this reduction in weight translates directly into less physical strain and greater independence.

Ergonomic Seating for Long-Term Comfort

Comfort is not an afterthought in the YSM2115—it is a core design principle. The chair provides multiple customization options including seat width options of 38 / 40 / 42 cm, a seat depth of 41 cm, and a backrest height adjustable between 45–55 cm.

These adjustable dimensions allow for a more tailored fit, helping users maintain proper posture and reduce pressure points during extended use.

Breathable Anti-Bedsore Cushion

The YSM2115 features a thickened breathable cushion designed to reduce the risk of pressure sores. This is especially beneficial for users who spend long periods seated, as it improves air circulation and distributes weight more evenly.

Sport-Inspired Butterfly Backrest Design

The wheelchair introduces a butterfly-shaped backrest design, which is both functional and visually distinctive. This structure supports improved upper back stability, enhanced posture alignment, and a more modern sporty aesthetic.

Unlike traditional rigid designs, the butterfly structure adds a dynamic touch while still prioritizing spinal support.

High-Performance Wheels for Smooth Mobility

Wheel configuration plays a critical role in performance. The YSM2115 is equipped with 24-inch rear wheels and 4-inch front casters, ensuring a balance between propulsion efficiency and maneuverability.

The large rear wheels enhance self-driven mobility, while the compact front wheels improve agility in tight indoor environments. This combination allows smooth transitions between indoor and outdoor surfaces.

Multi-Speed Handbrake System for Safety Control

Safety is reinforced through a multi-speed handbrake system, giving users and caregivers better control in various conditions.

It enables controlled deceleration on slopes, stable parking on uneven ground, and improved safety during transfers, making it especially valuable in urban environments.

Compact Yet Functional Structure

Despite its advanced features, the YSM2115 maintains a compact footprint with overall dimensions of 86 × 60 × 88 cm and a packaging size of 68 × 64 × 58 cm. It supports a load capacity of up to 100 kg.

This compact structure ensures easy navigation through standard doorways and elevators while maintaining reliable strength for everyday use.

Who Is the YSM2115 Designed For?

The YSM2115 is suitable for individuals seeking an active mobility solution, rehabilitation patients requiring transitional support, elderly users needing stable daily assistance, and caregivers who prioritize portability and ease of handling.

Its balance of lightweight structure and ergonomic design makes it suitable for both short-term recovery and long-term use.

Final Thoughts

The YSM2115 High-Performance Aluminum Sports Wheelchair represents a thoughtful combination of engineering and user-centered design. It focuses not only on mobility, but also on restoring confidence, independence, and ease of movement in daily life.

With its lightweight aluminum frame, ergonomic seating system, butterfly backrest, and responsive braking and wheel design, it delivers a well-rounded solution for users who value both comfort and performance.
