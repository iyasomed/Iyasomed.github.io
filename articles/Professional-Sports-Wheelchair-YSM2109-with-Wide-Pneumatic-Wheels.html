Professional Sports Wheelchair YSM2109 with Wide Pneumatic Wheels

Published on: 2026-5-07 | Author: Iyasocare

The YSM2109 Professional Sports Wheelchair is designed for users who value mobility, stability, and everyday practicality without sacrificing comfort. Built with a lightweight aluminum alloy frame and equipped with wide pneumatic rear wheels, this model aims to strike a balance between performance and usability in both indoor and outdoor environments.

Whether used for daily movement, recreational activity, or light sports applications, the YSM2109 focuses on delivering a reliable and adaptable mobility solution.

1. Frame Design: Lightweight Yet Strong

At the core of the YSM2109 is its aluminum alloy foldable frame, which offers a strong yet lightweight structure.

Material: Aluminum alloy
N/G Weight: 12.8 kg / 15.2 kg
Foldable design for easy transport and storage

The foldable structure allows users or caregivers to quickly collapse the wheelchair into a compact form. With a folded size of 87 × 33 × 76 cm, it becomes convenient for car trunks, storage rooms, or travel use.

Despite its relatively light weight, the frame is engineered to support up to 100 kg load capacity, making it suitable for a wide range of users.

2. Seating Comfort and Ergonomics

Comfort is a key factor in wheelchair design, especially for long-term use. The YSM2109 provides a thoughtfully structured seating system:

Seat width options: 36 / 38 / 40 / 42 cm
Seat depth: 37 cm
Seat height: 48 cm
Foam seat and back cushions

The multiple seat width options help users choose a more personalized fit, which is important for posture support and long-term comfort. The foam padding on both seat and backrest enhances pressure distribution and reduces fatigue during extended use.

3. Wheel System: Built for Stability and Versatility

The YSM2109 is designed with a wheel configuration that supports both indoor maneuverability and outdoor durability.

Rear Wheels: 24 inches, wide pneumatic quick-release wheels

The large rear wheels improve rolling efficiency and stability, especially on uneven terrain. Pneumatic tires provide better shock absorption compared to solid wheels, making outdoor travel smoother.

Front Wheels: 4 inches, wider solid design

The compact yet widened front wheels offer improved steering control and durability, making it easier to navigate tight indoor spaces and urban environments.

4. Armrests and Footrests: Practical Adjustability

The YSM2109 is designed with user convenience in mind:

Fixed armrests for structural stability
Foldable and height-adjustable footrests

The footrest system allows users to adjust leg positioning for comfort or fold it away for easier transfers in and out of the chair, supporting a wide range of mobility needs.

5. Size and Portability Overview

Product size: 84 × 60 × 77 cm
Folding size: 87 × 33 × 76 cm
Package size: 87 × 36 × 79 cm

These dimensions show that while the wheelchair maintains a sturdy full-use structure, it remains compact when folded, making it practical for travel and storage in limited spaces.

6. Performance in Everyday Use

The YSM2109 is built for practical real-world mobility, supporting a variety of daily scenarios:

Indoor navigation through narrow spaces
Outdoor travel over uneven surfaces
Short-distance transportation and daily errands
Recreational and light sports activities

The wide pneumatic rear wheels significantly improve shock absorption, while the aluminum frame ensures durability without excessive weight.

7. Key Strengths at a Glance

The YSM2109 stands out for its balanced combination of features:

Lightweight aluminum alloy foldable frame
Multiple seat width options for better fit
Comfortable foam cushioning
Large 24-inch pneumatic rear wheels
Adjustable and foldable footrests
Compact folding design for easy transport
100 kg weight capacity

Final Thoughts

The Professional Sports Wheelchair is a well-rounded mobility solution that combines comfort, portability, and durability. It is especially suitable for users who need a dependable wheelchair for both indoor and outdoor environments, without compromising on ease of transport or seating comfort.
