Height-Adjustable Folding Electric Wheelchair YSE314 | Comfort, Portability, and Independence

Published on: 2026-01-16 | Author: Iyasocare

In today’s world, mobility independence is not just about convenience—it affects quality of life and personal dignity. For seniors, rehabilitation patients, or anyone with limited mobility, a powerful, comfortable, and portable electric wheelchair is more than a tool—it’s a life-enhancing device.

The YSE314 Height-Adjustable Folding Electric Wheelchair combines advanced technology, comfort, and portability to help users regain freedom of movement. In this guide, we’ll explore its key features, real-life use cases, target users, comparisons, and buying tips.

1. Key Features of the YSE314

✅ Power Lift Seat: Reach Every Corner Independently

The standout feature of the YSE314 is its electric height-adjustable seat (Power Lift). With a simple control, the seat can be raised or lowered up to 20 cm, allowing users to:

  • Reach kitchen counters, dining tables, and office desks
  • Access car interiors or public transportation seats
  • Grab items from high cabinets or shelves

Real-Life Example: Retired teacher Mrs. Li now uses the YSE314 to reach kitchen cupboards without assistance, giving her independence and confidence in her daily routine.

✅ Single-Hand Joystick & Omni-Directional Front Wheels

  • Single-hand joystick control for easy steering
  • Omni-directional front wheels for tight spaces and smooth turns
  • Maximum speed: 6 km/h, safe and efficient

Whether at home, in a shopping mall, or an office, YSE314 provides a true sense of autonomous driving.

✅ Powerful & Safe

  • Dual 24V 180W motors for reliable performance
  • 20Ah lithium battery with approximately 15 km range
  • Supports up to 125 kg (275 lbs)
  • Electromagnetic brakes for safety
  • Handles slopes up to 12°, perfect for short outdoor trips

This ensures YSE314 is suitable for both indoor and light outdoor use, providing stability and confidence.

✅ One-Touch Folding: Compact & Portable

  • Folded dimensions: 75×59×69 cm
  • Easily fits into car trunks
  • Ideal for home storage, office spaces, or travel

Real-Life Example: Mr. Zhang enjoys weekend park visits. The YSE314 folds neatly into his SUV trunk, making it easy to travel freely without hassle.

4. Buying Guide & Tips

  • Battery Life: Consider your daily travel distance; spare batteries are recommended for long outings
  • Safety Features: Prioritize electromagnetic brakes, anti-tip systems, and sturdy frames
  • Warranty & Certification: Choose brands with certified products and reliable after-sales service
  • Accessories: Cushions, rain covers, and cup holders can enhance comfort and convenience

5. FAQ (Frequently Asked Questions)

  1. Who is the YSE314 suitable for?
    Those needing independent mobility, limited home space, or a wheelchair for both indoor and outdoor use.
  2. What is the folded weight of the wheelchair?
    Approximately 35–40 kg (77–88 lbs), manageable for most adults, but a vehicle or lift may be needed.
  3. What is the maximum weight the lift seat supports?
    Up to 125 kg (275 lbs), suitable for most adults.
  4. Can it be used for long outdoor trips?
    Suitable for short outdoor trips like parks, shopping, and community roads. For rugged or long-distance terrain, caution is advised.
