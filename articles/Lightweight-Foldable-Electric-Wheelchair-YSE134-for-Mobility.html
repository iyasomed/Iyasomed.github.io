YSE134 Lightweight Foldable Electric Wheelchair for Mobility

Published on: 2026-07-17 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility challenges should never limit a person’s ability to explore life, stay connected with family and friends, or enjoy daily activities with confidence. The YSE134 Lightweight Foldable Electric Wheelchair is designed to provide a perfect combination of power, portability, comfort, and safety, making it an ideal mobility solution for elderly users, people with disabilities, and anyone who needs reliable assistance for indoor and outdoor travel.

With its modern aluminum frame, intelligent electric control system, strong off-road capability, and foldable modular structure, the YSE134 offers a new level of convenience and independence for users seeking a reliable electric wheelchair solution.

Lightweight Aluminum Frame with Foldable Design

The YSE134 is built with a high-quality aluminum alloy frame, providing excellent strength while maintaining a lightweight structure. The wheelchair has a net weight of approximately 60KG and a gross weight of 70KG, while supporting users up to 100KG.

Its innovative modular foldable design allows the wheelchair to be easily disassembled and stored, making transportation simple and convenient. Whether traveling by car, storing at home, or taking it on outdoor trips, the YSE134 is designed to fit modern lifestyles.

The compact packing size with a wooden box (123.5 × 83.5 × 94.5CM) ensures safe transportation and protection during delivery.

Powerful Electric Performance for Multiple Environments

Equipped with strong electric drive motors and durable off-road wheels, the YSE134 provides reliable performance on different surfaces, including:

  • Pavement roads
  • Grass areas
  • Outdoor paths
  • Slightly uneven terrain

The wheelchair features:

  • 12-inch rear wheels
  • 8-inch front wheels
  • Maximum speed of 6KM/h
  • Driving range of 20–40KM
  • Climbing ability up to 15°

The powerful wheel system gives users greater freedom to move around independently without depending on others. Whether used indoors or outdoors, the YSE134 delivers stable and comfortable mobility performance.

Intelligent Lithium Battery System

The YSE134 uses a high-performance 24V lithium battery system, available in:

  • 24V 15Ah lithium battery
  • 24V 30Ah lithium battery

The lithium battery offers excellent durability, longer service life, and stable power output. A full charging cycle takes approximately 4–6 hours, allowing users to prepare the wheelchair conveniently for daily use.

With a driving range of up to 40 kilometers, users can enjoy longer outdoor activities, shopping trips, community visits, and recreational journeys with greater confidence and independence.

Universal Controller for Easy Operation

The YSE134 comes with a universal intelligent controller, designed for simple and comfortable operation.

Users can easily control:

  • Forward and reverse movement
  • Speed adjustment
  • Direction changes
  • Power functions

The controller provides smooth handling and allows users to operate the wheelchair independently with minimal effort, improving daily convenience and user confidence.

Safe and Stable Driving Experience

Safety is one of the most important features of the YSE134. The wheelchair is equipped with an electromagnetic braking system, providing quick and stable stopping performance. The braking distance is controlled within:

≤800mm

The advanced safety system includes:

Anti-Slip Slope Protection

The wheelchair features an anti-slip design that helps prevent backward sliding when moving on slopes, improving confidence and stability during outdoor use.

Safe Low-Speed Start System

The intelligent low-speed start function prevents sudden acceleration, reducing potential safety risks and providing a smoother starting experience for users.

Strong Obstacle-Crossing Ability

With an obstacle crossing height of up to:

≤80mm

The YSE134 can handle common outdoor challenges such as small steps, uneven pavement, and minor road obstacles, providing reliable performance across different environments.

Adjustable Electric Comfort System

Comfort is a key priority in the design of the YSE134. The wheelchair provides multiple electric adjustment functions to meet different user needs and improve long-term sitting comfort.

Electric Seat Adjustment

The seat can lift up to 45 degrees, allowing users to adjust their sitting position for improved comfort and easier posture changes.

Electric Backrest Adjustment

The backrest can recline up to 160 degrees, providing a comfortable resting position for relaxation, reading, or taking breaks during long periods of use.

Adjustable Footrest

The footrest can be adjusted up to 70 degrees, helping users find a more natural leg position and reducing pressure during extended sitting.

360° Rotating Seat Design

The innovative rotating seat allows:

  • 360° seat adjustment
  • Easier transfers
  • Flexible positioning
  • Improved convenience indoors

This feature is especially helpful for users who need additional support when getting on or off the wheelchair, making daily transfers safer and more convenient.

Comfortable Seating Design

The YSE134 comes with a comfortable black cushion designed for daily mobility needs. The ergonomic seating structure provides better support and helps users maintain a comfortable posture during extended use.

The seat specifications include:

  • Seat width: 450mm
  • Seat depth: 420mm
  • Seat height: 440mm
