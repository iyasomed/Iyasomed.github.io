Certified Quality and International Standards

One of the key reasons Iyasocare has gained global trust is its strict adherence to international safety and quality certifications. Every wheelchair model undergoes rigorous testing and complies with recognized standards, including:

  • CE Certification – Required for entry into European markets, ensuring compliance with safety, health, and environmental protection standards.
  • ISO 13485 – An international quality management system standard specifically for medical device manufacturers.
  • TÜV Certification – Independent verification of product safety and performance.
  • Certificate of Free Sale (CFS) – Proof that Iyasocare’s products are legally sold in the domestic market and eligible for export.
  • CIBG Registration – Authorizing the company to market medical devices in specific regulated markets.
  • CE-Scooter-DOC & CE-Electric-Wheelchair-DOC – Declaration of Conformity confirming compliance of electric scooters and wheelchairs with European directives.
  • Certificate of Conformity – Ensuring that exported products meet destination country regulations.
