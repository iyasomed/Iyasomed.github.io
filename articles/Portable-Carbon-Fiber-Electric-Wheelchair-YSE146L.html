Portable Carbon Fiber Electric Wheelchair YSE146L

Published on: 2026-03-11 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility is more than just moving from one place to another—it’s about independence, confidence, and freedom. For people with limited mobility, having a reliable, lightweight, and high-performance wheelchair can transform daily life. The YSE146L Portable Carbon Fiber Electric Wheelchair is designed with these needs in mind, combining cutting-edge technology, portability, and comfort for travel and everyday use.

Ultra-Lightweight Design for Maximum Portability

One of the standout features of the YSE146L is its carbon fiber frame, making the wheelchair exceptionally lightweight. Weighing just 11.5 kg without the battery and 16 kg with the battery, it’s easy to lift, carry, and store. Its folded size of 72 x 30 x 70 cm allows it to fit into car trunks, airplane compartments, or even compact storage spaces, making it an ideal choice for travelers and active users who want freedom without limits.

Comfortable, Ergonomic Seating

Comfort is crucial for a wheelchair user, and the YSE146L delivers. The seat measures 40 cm wide, 41 cm deep, with a 45 cm seat height, providing excellent support for prolonged use. A soft black cushion adds extra comfort, while flip-up armrests make transfers effortless and allow you to sit closer to tables or desks. Every detail is designed to enhance usability and comfort, whether at home, at work, or on the go.

Powerful Brushless Motors and Reliable Safety

The YSE146L is equipped with dual 24V 150W brushless motors and an electromagnetic parking brake, ensuring smooth and quiet operation. It can reach a top speed of 4.5 km/h and climb inclines of up to 3°, making it versatile for indoor and outdoor use. The wheelchair provides a safe and responsive ride, with a reliable braking system for added peace of mind.

Long-Lasting Battery and Efficient Charging

The wheelchair’s 24V 10Ah lithium battery is lightweight at just 2 kg, yet powerful enough to cover 10-15 km on a single charge. Charging takes only 4-6 hours, allowing users to enjoy longer outings without worry. Whether you’re shopping, commuting, or exploring, the YSE146L ensures you have the energy and freedom you need.

Durable Wheels for Smooth Rides

Equipped with 11-inch rear wheels and 6.5-inch front wheels made from durable PU material, the YSE146L handles various surfaces comfortably and reliably. Its carbon fiber frame combines strength and lightness, supporting up to 100 kg safely while remaining corrosion-resistant and long-lasting.

Perfect for Travel and Daily Life

Whether you’re navigating crowded city streets, taking public transportation, or traveling for leisure, the YSE146L’s compact, foldable design makes it a perfect travel companion. Its portability doesn’t compromise performance—users can enjoy a smooth, safe, and independent ride wherever they go.

Eco-Friendly and Quiet Operation

With brushless motors and a lithium battery, the YSE146L operates quietly and produces zero emissions. It’s a sustainable choice for environmentally conscious users who want reliable mobility without noise or pollution.

Why Choose the YSE146L?

The YSE146L Portable Carbon Fiber Electric Wheelchair blends lightweight portability, powerful performance, ergonomic design, and modern technology. It’s ideal for daily use, travel, or any activity where mobility and independence matter. Choosing the YSE146L means choosing comfort, freedom, and reliability—empowering users to move confidently through life.
