Foldable Sports Wheelchair YSM2102 Designed for Everyday Freedom

Published on: 2026-04-22 | Author: Iyasocare

The YSM2102 Lightweight Foldable Sports Wheelchair is designed for users who need a practical balance between portability, durability, and safety. Built with an aviation-grade aluminum alloy frame and equipped with quick-release wheels and anti-tip protection, it focuses on making daily mobility simpler and more flexible—whether at home, outdoors, or during travel.

Below is a complete breakdown of its structure, features, and real-world usability.

Compact Structure for Easy Storage and Transport

The YSM2102 adopts a space-saving design that makes transportation significantly easier.

Seat width: 36 cm
Seat depth: 48 cm
Seat height: 48 cm
Overall dimensions: 85 × 56 × 77 cm
Folded dimensions: 85 × 34 × 77 cm

Once folded, the wheelchair becomes significantly slimmer, allowing it to fit into most car trunks without difficulty. This makes it especially practical for users who frequently travel or need to store the chair in limited spaces.

Aviation-Grade Aluminum Alloy Frame

At the core of the YSM2102 is a high-strength aluminum alloy frame, designed to reduce weight while maintaining structural reliability.

Key advantages:
Lightweight but durable construction
Corrosion-resistant for long-term use
Stable support under daily load conditions

Weight specifications:
Net weight: 11.6 kg
Gross weight: 14 kg
Maximum load capacity: 100 kg

This balance allows the wheelchair to remain easy to handle while still supporting everyday mobility needs.

Quick Folding Design for Daily Convenience

The wheelchair features a simple folding mechanism that requires minimal effort.

Benefits include:
Fast folding and unfolding
No tools required
Easy storage in vehicles or tight spaces
Suitable for travel and daily commuting

This design significantly reduces the hassle of transportation and storage, making it highly user-friendly for both individuals and caregivers.

Quick-Release Rear Wheels for Better Portability

A standout feature of the YSM2102 is its quick-release rear wheel system.

How it works:
Press the release button
Rear wheels detach instantly

Advantages:
Reduces total carrying weight
Easier loading into vehicles
Simplified maintenance and cleaning

This feature is particularly useful in situations where space and lifting effort are limited.

Wheel Configuration for Indoor and Outdoor Use

The YSM2102 is equipped with a practical wheel setup designed for different environments.

Rear wheels:
Size: 24 inches
Provide strong propulsion efficiency
Suitable for outdoor travel and longer distances

Front wheels:
Size: 4 inches
Offer smooth turning control
Ideal for indoor and tight spaces

This combination ensures smooth performance whether indoors or outdoors.

Anti-Tip Safety System for Stability

Safety is an essential part of wheelchair design, and the YSM2102 includes dual rear anti-tip wheels.

Safety benefits:
Prevents backward tipping on slopes
Enhances stability on uneven terrain
Increases user confidence during outdoor use

This feature is especially valuable for users navigating ramps or outdoor environments.

Everyday Performance and User Experience

The YSM2102 focuses on practicality and ease of use rather than unnecessary complexity.

Core strengths:
Lightweight aluminum construction
Compact folding design for travel convenience
Quick-release rear wheel system
Large rear wheels for efficient movement
Anti-tip stability support
Suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments

It is designed to make daily mobility smoother, not more complicated.

Recommended For

This model is suitable for:

Individuals requiring daily mobility support
Elderly users needing reliable assistance
Rehabilitation and recovery users
Caregivers who need easy transport and handling
Users who travel frequently and need portability

Conclusion

The YSM2102 Foldable Sports Wheelchair is built around a simple but important idea: making mobility easier and more accessible. It achieves this through lightweight materials, compact folding, and thoughtful safety features.

Its design philosophy can be summed up as:

Practical, portable, and dependable—built for everyday freedom of movement.

For users seeking a straightforward, travel-friendly manual wheelchair that performs well in multiple environments, the YSM2102 offers a solid and reliable solution.
