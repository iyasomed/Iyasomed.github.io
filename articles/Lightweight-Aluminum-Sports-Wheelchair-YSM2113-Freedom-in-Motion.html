Lightweight Aluminum Sports Wheelchair YSM2113 – Freedom in Motion

Published on: 2026-5-14 | Author: Iyasocare

In the evolving world of mobility solutions, the YSM2113 Sports Wheelchair stands as a refined balance between engineering precision, comfort, and athletic functionality. Designed with a lightweight aluminum frame and a thoughtful ergonomic structure, it is built not only to assist mobility but to empower independence, speed, and active living.

At the core of the YSM2113 is its ultralight aluminum frame, carefully engineered to reduce overall weight while maintaining structural strength and durability. Weighing approximately 12.9 kg net (15.5 kg gross), this wheelchair is significantly lighter than traditional models, making it easier to transport, store, and maneuver.

Its foldable structure further enhances portability, collapsing into a compact size of 85 × 28 × 88 cm, allowing users and caregivers to easily place it in car trunks or storage spaces without hassle.

1. Adjustable Fit and Ergonomic Design

The YSM2113 is designed with adaptability in mind. It offers multiple seat width options—36 cm, 38 cm, and 40 cm—ensuring a tailored fit for different body types.

The seat depth of 40 cm and seat height of 50 cm provide a stable and balanced sitting posture, while the 33 cm backrest height supports the upper back without restricting movement.

A distinctive feature is its butterfly-shaped backrest, designed to contour naturally to the user’s spine, offering enhanced comfort during long periods of use and reducing pressure fatigue.

2. Comfort-Oriented Materials

Comfort is further reinforced through its seating materials. The combination of breathable Oxford canvas and high-density sponge cushioning ensures both ventilation and softness.

This helps prevent discomfort caused by heat or prolonged sitting, making it suitable for both indoor and outdoor activities.

3. Wheel System and Performance

The YSM2113 is equipped with 24-inch inflatable rear sports wheels and 4-inch PU front wheels, creating a smooth and responsive riding experience.

The large rear wheels provide excellent propulsion and stability, especially for active users or sports applications, while the PU front wheels offer durability and shock absorption on various surfaces.

Whether navigating city pavements, indoor arenas, or outdoor tracks, the wheelchair maintains consistent control and agility.

4. Structure and Load Capacity

The frame supports a maximum load capacity of 100 kg, making it suitable for a wide range of users.

The overall product size of 85 × 55 × 89 cm strikes a balance between compact design and user comfort, ensuring maneuverability without sacrificing space.

5. Mobility Freedom Philosophy

Beyond specifications, the YSM2113 embodies a philosophy of mobility freedom. It is designed for individuals who seek not just transportation, but participation—those who value independence in sports, travel, and daily life.

The lightweight structure reduces physical strain on caregivers, while the foldable design enhances convenience for transportation and storage.

Final Thoughts

The YSM2113 Lightweight Aluminum Sports Wheelchair is more than a mobility aid; it is a performance-oriented companion. It merges durability, comfort, and athletic responsiveness into a single design.

Every component—from its aluminum frame to its breathable cushion system—has been carefully selected to support a dynamic, active lifestyle.

For users seeking reliability, comfort, and mobility freedom, the YSM2113 represents a modern solution that transforms everyday movement into effortless motion.
