Discover the YSE154 Foldable Electric Wheelchair: Convenience Meets Affordability

Published on: 2026-01-28 | Author: Iyasocare

Mobility should never be a barrier to independence, and the YSE154 Foldable Electric Wheelchair makes that easier than ever. Designed for comfort, flexibility, and portability, this wheelchair is perfect for those seeking both functionality and convenience at a budget-friendly price.

Compact Yet Comfortable Design

The YSE154 features a thoughtfully designed seat measuring 43cm wide, 44cm deep, and 51cm high, offering a comfortable fit for everyday use. Despite its compact size, the wheelchair supports a load of up to 100kg, making it a reliable choice for a wide range of users.

With an overall product size of 102 × 67 × 89cm, it’s roomy enough for comfort, yet the clever folding feature reduces it to 70 × 42 × 73cm, making storage and transport a breeze. Whether traveling by car or storing it at home, this wheelchair adapts to your lifestyle.

Key Features for Maximum Convenience

The YSE154 is packed with user-friendly features:

  • Swivel Armrests: Armrests can be turned over for easier transfers in and out of the wheelchair.
  • Flip-Up Footrests: Pedals can also be turned over, providing more legroom and accessibility.
  • Quick-Release Battery: The battery can be removed effortlessly for charging or replacement, saving time and effort.
  • Adjustable Controller: The joystick controller can be moved forward or backward and swapped left to right, ensuring maximum comfort and ease of use.

Smooth and Sturdy Performance

Equipped with 12-inch rear wheels and 8-inch front wheels, the YSE154 ensures smooth navigation both indoors and on outdoor surfaces. The steel frame provides stability and durability without compromising portability.

Weighing only 33kg (net) and 36kg (gross), this wheelchair is light enough for easy lifting, yet strong enough to handle daily mobility needs.

Ideal for Active, On-the-Go Lifestyles

Whether you’re heading out for a day trip, navigating tight indoor spaces, or storing it in a vehicle for travel, the YSE154 excels in versatility. Its foldable design and practical features make it perfect for seniors, people with disabilities, or anyone who values independent mobility.

Specifications at a Glance:

  • Seat: 43cm W × 44cm D × 51cm H
  • Product Size: 102 × 67 × 89cm
  • Folding Size: 70 × 42 × 73cm
  • Rear/Front Wheels: 12"/8"
  • Weight: 33kg (Net) / 36kg (Gross)
  • Frame Material: Steel
  • Load Capacity: 100kg
  • Packing Size: 75 × 34 × 76cm

The YSE154 Foldable Electric Wheelchair proves that comfort, convenience, and affordability can go hand in hand. If you’re looking for a wheelchair that adapts to your lifestyle, this is the perfect choice.
