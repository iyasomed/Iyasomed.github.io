Folding Sport Wheelchair YSM2107 Quick Release Wheels

Published on: 2026-4-28 | Author: Iyasocare

In recent years, mobility aid design has increasingly focused on lightweight structure, portability, and user convenience. For individuals who need occasional or daily mobility support—whether at home, in hospitals, or while traveling—a wheelchair that is easy to fold, lift, and store can significantly improve independence and quality of life.

The YSM2107 Folding Sport Wheelchair (Quick Release Wheels) is designed with exactly these priorities in mind. Below is a detailed breakdown of its structure, features, and real-world usability.

1. Overall Design Philosophy: Lightweight Yet Durable

The YSM2107 is built with an aluminum alloy frame, ensuring a strong structure while maintaining a lightweight profile.

Key weight specifications:

  • Net weight: 9.5 kg
  • Gross weight: 11.8 kg

Main advantages:

  • Easier lifting and carrying
  • Convenient for travel and transportation
  • Reduced physical strain for caregivers
  • Quick folding and storage

This balance between strength and portability makes it suitable for both indoor and short-distance outdoor use.

2. Compact Dimensions for Practical Mobility

The wheelchair is designed with a compact footprint while maintaining functional seating space.

  • Seat width: 36 cm
  • Seat height: 48 cm
  • Overall product size: 75 × 67 × 77 cm
  • Packaging size: 76 × 62 × 50 cm

This compact structure is ideal for maneuvering in narrow indoor environments such as hallways, elevators, and small rooms.

3. Wheel System: Quick-Release Rear Wheels

A key feature of the YSM2107 is its quick-release rear wheel system, designed for maximum convenience.

  • Rear wheels: 24 inches
  • Front wheels: 4 inches

Advantages of quick-release design:

  • Rear wheels can be removed without tools
  • Significantly reduces folded size
  • Easier storage in car trunks
  • Simplifies cleaning and maintenance

This system is especially useful for frequent travelers or users requiring compact storage solutions.

4. Safety and Comfort Features

Safety Belt
A built-in seat belt helps secure the user during movement, improving stability and reducing slipping risks.

Durable Footrests
The wheelchair uses sturdy plastic footboards designed to:

  • Support lower limbs securely
  • Provide stable resting positioning
  • Allow easy cleaning and maintenance
  • Reduce overall weight

These components balance practicality, durability, and lightweight construction.

5. Load Capacity and Usage Scope

The YSM2107 supports a maximum load of 100 kg, making it suitable for most adult users.

Recommended usage scenarios:

  • Daily home mobility assistance
  • Hospital and rehabilitation environments
  • Temporary injury recovery support
  • Short trips and travel use
  • Outdoor visits and transportation

It is best categorized as a lightweight manual wheelchair optimized for portability rather than heavy-duty continuous outdoor use.

6. Foldability and Transport Convenience

Thanks to its aluminum frame and detachable wheel system, the YSM2107 offers excellent storage efficiency.

  • Quick folding mechanism
  • Reduced size after wheel removal
  • Fits easily into most car trunks
  • Convenient for travel and logistics

This makes it especially suitable for families who frequently travel or move between locations.

7. Overall Evaluation

Key strengths:

  • Lightweight aluminum alloy frame
  • Quick-release rear wheels for easy transport
  • Compact folding structure
  • Essential safety features included
  • Suitable for home and travel use

Limitations:

  • Relatively narrow seat width (36 cm)
  • Focuses on basic functionality rather than premium comfort
  • Not intended for heavy-duty or long-term intensive outdoor use

Conclusion

The YSM2107 Folding Sport Wheelchair is designed for users who prioritize lightweight construction, portability, and everyday convenience. It performs well within its intended category rather than as a luxury or heavy-duty model.

For home use, short trips, rehabilitation needs, or travel situations, this wheelchair provides a practical and efficient mobility solution. Its quick-release wheels and foldable design make it especially appealing for users who need a balance between functionality and easy transportation.
