Discover Freedom with the YSE131 Electric Wheelchair

Published on: 2026-02-12 | Author: Iyasocare

For many people, mobility can be a daily challenge. The right wheelchair, however, can transform independence, comfort, and confidence. The YSE131 Electric Wheelchair is designed to make everyday life easier—whether at home, in the community, or on the go.

Compact, Lightweight, and Travel-Friendly

The YSE131’s foldable design makes it perfect for travel and storage. Weighing only 44.5 kg and folding down to 72 × 38 × 74 cm, it fits easily into car trunks, closets, or tight spaces. With this wheelchair, taking your independence on the road has never been easier.

Comfortable Seating for Larger Users

Comfort is key, and the YSE131 delivers. With a 45 cm wide seat and 45 cm depth, this wheelchair offers plenty of room for larger users. The adjustable footrest and removable armrests allow for a personalized seating experience, making every ride smooth and relaxed.

Powerful Performance & Easy Climbing

Equipped with dual 250W motors, the YSE131 can reach speeds up to 6 km/h and cover distances up to 15 km on a single charge. Its powerful motors handle slopes of up to 15°, ensuring that hills and inclines are no longer obstacles to your mobility.

Safety You Can Rely On

The YSE131 features a dual braking system—manual brakes plus an electronic parking brake—giving you peace of mind wherever you go. Whether stopping on a flat surface or a steep incline, you can trust your wheelchair to stay secure.

Stylish and Versatile

Available in Black, Red, and Blue, the YSE131 combines durable steel construction with comfortable cushions, creating a wheelchair that’s both practical and modern. Its sleek design ensures you move in style.

Quick Specs at a Glance

  • Seat Width/Depth/Height: 45 cm / 45 cm / 50 cm
  • Backrest Height: 39 cm
  • Overall Size: 118 × 63 × 93 cm
  • Front/Rear Wheel Size: 10 in / 16 in
  • Battery & Charging: 24V 12Ah lead-acid, 4–6 hours charging
  • Max Load: 130 kg
  • Motors: 250W × 2
  • Top Speed: 6 km/h
  • Climbing Ability: 15°

Why Choose the YSE131?

The YSE131 is more than just a wheelchair—it’s a ticket to independence. Its lightweight portability, spacious seating, strong motor performance, and reliable safety features make it perfect for anyone looking to regain freedom of movement.

Take control of your mobility with the YSE131 Electric Wheelchair—comfortable, convenient, and built for life on the go.
