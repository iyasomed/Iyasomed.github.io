Electric Wheelchair YSE141 Compact, Foldable, and Comfortable Mobility

Published on: 2026-03-06 | Author: Iyasocare

In today’s world, personal mobility is more than convenience—it’s about independence, comfort, and confidence. The YSE141 electric wheelchair combines lightweight design, advanced technology, and ergonomic comfort, providing users with a reliable solution for both indoor and outdoor mobility. With its thoughtful engineering, this wheelchair empowers users to navigate daily life with ease and freedom.

Ergonomic Seating for Maximum Comfort

The YSE141 features a spacious and supportive seat, with a seat width of 420mm, seat depth of 430mm, and seat height of 500mm, paired with a 480mm high backrest. This design ensures stable support and a comfortable posture for prolonged use.

The wheelchair is equipped with dual-layer compound cushions available in black, gold, or red, offering both softness and stability. Additionally, the flip-up footrest allows users to adjust leg positioning according to their comfort, preventing fatigue during longer trips or extended use.

Lightweight Aluminum Frame for Portability

Built from high-strength aluminum alloy, the YSE141 achieves the perfect balance between durability and weight. The wheelchair weighs just 27kg net, 32kg gross, and folds down to 680 × 270 × 836mm, making it easy to store in car trunks or compact spaces at home. When fully unfolded, the wheelchair measures 1040 × 680 × 836mm, providing a stable and secure ride while supporting users up to 100kg.

Dual-Motor Drive: Power and Range

Equipped with two 350W brushless motors, the YSE141 delivers smooth, reliable performance, reaching speeds up to 6 km/h and a driving range of 10–20 km per charge. The wheelchair can handle inclines up to 9°, making ramps and slopes easy to navigate.

The 24V 12Ah lithium battery charges in just 4–6 hours, ensuring that the wheelchair is ready for use with minimal downtime. This battery provides consistent and energy-efficient power for daily mobility needs.

Safety and Precise Control

Safety is a top priority. The YSE141 features electromagnetic brakes and a lengthened metal manual brake, providing a stopping distance of ≤1000mm for secure operation.

The 360° joystick brushless controller allows precise steering and maneuverability, even in tight spaces. Combined with obstacle clearance of up to 40mm, users can confidently navigate indoor thresholds, sidewalks, and minor uneven surfaces.

Optimized Wheels for Low Maintenance

The wheelchair features 8-inch solid rubber front wheels and 10-inch non-inflatable rear wheels, paired with dual brushless motors. This wheel setup ensures stability, durability, and minimal maintenance, while providing a smooth and comfortable ride across different terrains.

Thoughtful Features for Everyday Use

  • Foldable aluminum frame for easy storage and transport.
  • Dual-layer compound cushion for ergonomic comfort.
  • Flip-up footrest for customizable positioning.
  • Lengthened metal manual brake for enhanced safety.
  • 360° joystick brushless controller for precise control.
  • Durable front and rear wheels for reliable performance and low maintenance.

Conclusion

The YSE141 electric wheelchair is a modern mobility solution that combines lightweight design, comfort, and intelligent performance. Ideal for seniors, individuals with limited mobility, or anyone seeking a reliable personal transport device, it ensures safe, smooth, and independent travel.

With its dual-motor system, long-lasting lithium battery, foldable frame, and comprehensive safety features, the YSE141 gives users freedom, independence, and peace of mind. Whether moving indoors, running errands, or enjoying short outdoor trips, this wheelchair delivers unmatched comfort, stability, and convenience.
